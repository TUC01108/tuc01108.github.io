Bio


Born in 1991 outside Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA, Thomas enjoyed things that involved puzzles and strategy from an early age. Chess was his first experience of going head to head with another individual and without memorizing moves he found success against his opponents by following natural flow. Fast forward to 2020 and he has refound his passion of puzzles and challenges by focusing on computer science and web development. He sees the emergence of bitcoin and digital assets as unlocking the floodgates of how money is meant to flow through society. He believes micro-payments and paying someone by the second is the new way of things and with reduced friction and increased liquidity we will see a new world unfold very quickly. First he needs to master C, Python, and all the things that will help him to actually make an impact on this new spark for the internet of value.
