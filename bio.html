Bio


Born in 1991 outside Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA, Thomas enjoyed things that involved puzzles and strategy from an early age. Chess was his first memorable experience of going head to head with another individual and without memorizing moves he found success against his opponents by following natural flow and recognizing patterns unfolding. Fast forward to the present and he has refound his passion of puzzles and challenges by focusing on computer engineering. He lives with his wife and daughter in Los Angeles, CA and loves spending time at the beach and eating yummy food with them.
